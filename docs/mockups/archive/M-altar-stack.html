Of the Rite

Upon this page rests the manner of consultation. The circle above thee is the locus — a throat through which the grimoire may speak.

I. Set the Needle

The south-pointing tip names thy temper: from Tactical to Chaotic.

The Utterance
II. Inscribe

Upon the scrap held within the circle, write thy question plainly.

III. Commit

Speak the word "Consult" and the grimoire shall answer with five workings drawn from the art. Re-turn the needle to re-shape the counsel.
