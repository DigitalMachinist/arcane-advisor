❦ On the Matter of Spells ❦

A wise mage does not merely memorise incantations, but studies their nature — the weight of each word, the flavour of each gesture, the hour at which they sing most true. Upon these pages you will inscribe your need, and from the archive shall be drawn such spells as suit your hour.

Speak plainly of what you face, or speak in riddles — the book attends to both. Tell of collapsing mines or of libraries, of dragons upon volcanoes or of banquets in kings' halls. The archive listens.
