Whimsy Dial

Five runes in a horizontal row, each in its canonical Spec 6 palette. The active rune controls the whimsy applied to the next prompt: brighter stroke, outer glow, label elevated out of dimness. In the live UI the dial sits immediately beneath the prompt dock and drives both the casting-circle colour cascade and the compass needle direction.
