At the top of the right-hand page you will find a scale, inked across the margin and illuminated at one of its five stations. The lit point is the voice in which the tome will reply.

Touch another station to move the light. The ink remains; the illumination wanders. The tome remembers which station you favoured and returns there on your next visit.

Set the station. Ink your question. Turn the page.
