The Rite

Set the needle of the compass upon the sigil that matches the temper of the rite thou wouldst perform.

I. Turn the Dial

The southern tip seeks the flavour thou desirest — from Tactical plain to Chaotic wild.

II. Inscribe

Upon the scrap within the circle, write the question of thine heart: the foe, the puzzle, the hour.

III. Commit

Speak the word "Consult," and the grimoire shall answer with five workings drawn from the art.
