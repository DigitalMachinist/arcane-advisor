❦ Of the Art of Asking ❦

A wise mage does not merely memorise incantations, but studies their nature — the weight of each word, the flavour of each gesture, the hour at which they sing most true. Upon the right-hand page you shall inscribe your need, and from the archive shall be drawn such spells as fit the moment.

Speak plainly of the matter before you, or speak in riddles — the book attends to both. Tell of collapsing mines, of libraries, of dragons upon volcanoes, of banquets in kings' halls, or simply of a mood you cannot yet name.
