Transcript Turns

The two halves of every round: the user's prompt (rendered as a torn scrap anchored to the right half of the column) and the optional set-header that introduces the assistant's response. Also includes the round-header rule that separates rounds, and the minor divider used within a round.

User prompt scrap — three content lengths
Mini scrap

The scrap expands vertically to fit its content. The meta row (round number + whimsy badge) hangs under the right edge. Rotation is fixed — the scrap never tilts.

Round header & in-round divider
Separators

The round-header rule marks the boundary between two rounds and names the whimsy. The slim turn-divider is reserved for when a round is further broken into sub-sections (e.g. error card followed by retry skeleton).
