Rules Q&A answer
Meta lookup

Returned when the user asks a rules or mechanics question rather than requesting a spell recommendation. No cards follow — the banner is the entire system turn.

Rules question — Concentration (PHB 203) Being prone doesn't break concentration by itself — you can keep concentrating while lying on the ground. Concentration only breaks when you take damage and fail a Constitution save, when you're incapacitated, or when you die. So if being prone means you're hit hard enough to fail the Con save, yes — but that's the damage, not the prone condition.
